Girl Does Sports

Hannah Barrett is a Jill of All Trades but a Master of None. She has certainly done her fair share of dabbling when it comes to sports and exercise.

This website is where she shares her experience of falling back in love with sports, exercise and looking after herself after a few lazy years.
She is a strong believer in pushing yourself to try new things. You don't have to BE the best, you just have to do YOUR best!
